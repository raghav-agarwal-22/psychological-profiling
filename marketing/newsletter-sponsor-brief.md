## What is Innermind?

**Innermind** is an AI-powered psychological profiling platform that synthesizes five validated assessment frameworks into a single, personalized portrait — then helps you grow from it.

Most personality tools give you one answer. Innermind gives you five perspectives, synthesized by AI, so you finally understand the full picture of who you are.

## The Five Frameworks

| Framework | What It Reveals |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Big Five (OCEAN)** | Core personality dimensions: openness, conscientiousness, extraversion, agreeableness, neuroticism |
| **Schwartz Values** | Your hierarchy of motivating values — what you're actually optimizing for |
| **Attachment Style** | How you connect in relationships — the hidden driver of interpersonal behavior |
| **Enneagram** | Core motivations, fears, and growth edges |
| **Jungian Archetypes** | The deep psychological patterns that shape perception and identity |
