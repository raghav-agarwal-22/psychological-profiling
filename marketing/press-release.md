## Innermind Launches AI-Powered Psychological Profiling Platform That Synthesizes Five Assessment Frameworks Into One Personalized Portrait

*New platform combines Big Five, Schwartz Values, Attachment Style, Enneagram, and Jungian Archetypes with AI-generated insights and a psychology-aware coaching assistant*

**San Francisco, CA — March 2026** — Innermind, a psychological profiling platform, today announced its public launch with a product designed to give individuals deeper, more actionable self-knowledge than any single personality test can provide.

Unlike existing tools that assess personality through a single lens — such as Myers-Briggs or the Enneagram alone — Innermind runs users through five validated psychological assessment frameworks and uses Claude AI to synthesize the results into a unified, personalized psychological portrait. The platform then enables ongoing growth tracking, a reflection journal, and an AI coaching assistant grounded in each user's specific profile.

---

## A New Standard for Psychological Self-Knowledge

The self-help and coaching industry has long relied on single-framework personality tests that, while popular, provide limited predictive validity and cannot capture the complexity of human psychology. Research consistently shows that psychological outcomes — from career satisfaction to relationship health to leadership effectiveness — are best predicted by multiple dimensions in combination.

"We built Innermind because one framework was never going to be enough," said [Founder Name], founder and CEO of Innermind. "When you understand your Big Five trait profile alongside your core values, your attachment patterns, and your Enneagram type, you stop seeing yourself as a label and start understanding the system that drives your behavior. That's the insight people actually need."

Innermind's platform includes:

- **Five validated assessments** covering Big Five personality traits (OCEAN), Schwartz Values Theory, Attachment Style, Enneagram, and Jungian Archetypes
- **AI-synthesized psychological portrait** generated by Claude, connecting patterns across all five frameworks into a coherent, personalized narrative
- **Growth tracking** that allows users to retake assessments over time and visualize score changes
- **Adaptive deep-dive questions** — AI-generated follow-up questions tailored to each user's profile
- **Reflection journal** for linking psychological insights to real-life experiences
- **AI coach** — a conversational coaching assistant that knows the user's complete psychological profile

## Market Context

The global personality assessment market is valued at over $2.5 billion and growing at 12% annually, driven by demand from HR teams, executive coaches, and individuals seeking personal development tools. Despite this growth, research by organizational psychologist Tasha Eurich estimates that only 10-15% of people are genuinely self-aware — creating a substantial gap between available tools and measurable impact.

Innermind targets this gap by combining the scientific rigor of validated assessment instruments with the synthesis and conversational capabilities of modern AI.

The platform is currently available direct-to-consumer at innermindhealing.com, with plans for B2B offerings for executive coaches, HR teams, and corporate learning and development programs.
