## Subject Line Performance Notes

- "Earn recurring income" outperforms "partnership" in cold outreach open rates (income framing = self-interest hook)
- Including the channel name in subject boosts open rate ~20% vs. generic
- Avoid "collab" or "sponsor" in subject — implies upfront cost, which this isn't
- Best send time: Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am creator's local time
