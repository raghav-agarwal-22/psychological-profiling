## Selection Criteria

Scored each post across three axes (1–10 each):
- **Volume** — monthly search volume for the primary keyword
- **Low Competition** — how easy is it to beat current page-1 results?
- **Fit** — how well does Innermind's multi-framework approach differentiate us?

Top 10 = highest combined score.
