## The Problem

Most people are psychologically illiterate about themselves — not for lack of curiosity, but lack of tools. Existing personality tests (16Personalities, MBTI, online quizzes) deliver labels without depth: you're an "INTJ" or a "Type 4," but you have no idea what that means for how you love, work, conflict, or grow. No platform synthesizes multiple validated frameworks into a single coherent portrait of who you actually are.

---

## The Solution

**Innermind** is an AI-powered psychological profiling platform that combines 6 validated frameworks — Big Five, Enneagram, Attachment Style, Jungian Archetypes, Moral Foundations, and Schwartz Values — into a single AI-written portrait that reads like it was written by a psychologist who knows you well.

Free tier: take the Big Five assessment, see a partial profile. Pro ($19/mo): all 6 frameworks, a full synthesized portrait, adaptive deep-dive conversations, an AI coach, and growth tracking over time.
