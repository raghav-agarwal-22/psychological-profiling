## Engagement Playbook

### Daily Routine (20–30 min)
1. **Search terms to monitor:** "personality test", "MBTI", "Enneagram", "Big Five", "attachment style", "shadow work", "psychological type"
2. **Reply rule:** Only reply when you can add genuine value. Don't spam with Innermind links.
3. **Reply formula:**
   - Lead with insight or agree with something specific
   - Add a layer of depth ("What's interesting is...")
   - Optional: light CTA only if deeply relevant ("We built something that takes this further — [link]")

### What to Reply To
- "What personality test should I take?" → Recommend Big Five first, explain why vs MBTI
- "I'm an INFP/INFJ/4w5" → Engage authentically on the framework, mention Innermind naturally
- "Anyone else [anxiety pattern / attachment behavior]?" → Normalize with psychology context
- "I don't know who I am anymore" → Point to self-knowledge frameworks

### What NOT to Do
- Don't drop Innermind link on every reply
- Don't post the same thread twice in one week
- Don't auto-DM followers (trust killer)
- Don't engage with politics, controversy, or drama
- Don't post more than 3x per day (quality > volume)

### Virality Mechanics
- **Polls** get native amplification on X — use them weekly
- **Thread openers must stand alone** as a screenshot — no context assumed
- **End threads with a question** — drives reply engagement which boosts distribution
- **Archetype share cards** (INN-99 built feature) — encourage users to share their results on X
- **Retweet your own threads** at 6pm if morning thread underperformed
