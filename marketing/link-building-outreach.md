## Response Handling

**No response after 7 days:** Send one follow-up. Subject: "Quick follow-up on [POST TITLE]". Keep it to 2 sentences.

**Positive response:** Offer to write a custom excerpt or blurb for their page. Move fast — respond within 24 hours.

**"We charge for links":** Skip. Paid links violate Google guidelines and create long-term risk.

**"We'd love a guest post":** Use this as a link opportunity. Write a 600–800 word post on an adjacent topic with one contextual link back to the target post.
