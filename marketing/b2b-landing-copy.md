## How It Works

**How it works for your practice**

01. **You sign up and get a client invite link**
    Create a free account and share your unique invite link with clients via email or messaging. They sign up and start assessments immediately — no back-and-forth scheduling required.

02. **Clients complete five 15-minute assessments**
    Each client completes Big Five, Schwartz Values, Attachment Style, Enneagram, and Jungian Archetypes at their own pace. Innermind synthesizes results into a readable AI portrait.

03. **Clients share their portrait with you**
    With one click, clients grant you access to their full profile: raw scores, AI synthesis, and growth chart. You can review it before your first session.

04. **Track progress together over time**
    Clients retake assessments quarterly. You see score changes mapped to your coaching work — a timeline of their inner growth that you can reference in sessions.
